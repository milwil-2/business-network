Math 54 develops linear algebra and differential equations side by side. The linear-algebra portion covers systems of linear equations, matrix operations, vector spaces, bases, linear transformations, determinants, eigenvalues, and eigenvectors.

These tools are then applied to differential equations: solving linear systems of ODEs, using eigen-decomposition to diagonalize, and studying oscillations via the matrix exponential. The course also introduces orthogonality, least squares, and the spectral theorem.

The linear-algebra foundations from Math 54 are essential downstream, serving as a prerequisite for [[cs-189]] and the systems course [[eecs-16b]].
